KPIs: Total Sales ($899,902,125), Total Profit ($332,134,761.45), Sales Growth, and Top-Performing Products.

Breakdowns: Sales by product category, region, and year (2020 vs. 2021).

Visualizations: Line chart for sales trends, bar charts for sales by product, and a map for regional sales distribution.

This dashboard helps decision-makers track sales performance, identify top-performing products, and analyze regional trends across 2020 and 2021 with interactive visualizations.
